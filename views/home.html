Civility International

Mission

Civility International is a non-profit organization that focus on promoting, advocating, and empowering young leaders across the United States to create a culture of civility and acceptance for everyone to learn, develop, and thrive. Through helping create and support campus wide civility organizations, Civility Global plans on changing the worlds culture.

Project Civility

Project Civility's Mission

Project Civility is an initiative conceived by the Hamilton campus Student Government Association (SGA) in response to rude, disrespectful and hurtful comments and behaviors demonstrated by students at the beginning of the 2011-12 academic year. The organization is intended to foster climates and cultures at Miami University and around the world where civility, respect, and understanding are essential to the communities in which we live and serve. The initiative requires an on-going effort as we strive to make the world feel friendly, welcoming, and accepting of everyone.
